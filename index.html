Yu Haibin

I am a senior research engineer at Tencent now. Previously I was a research fellow at the School of Computing Department, National University of Singapore. It is my great honor to be co-supervised by Prof. Kian Hsiang Low and Prof. Patrick Jaillet. I obtained my bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering and Automation Department, Beihang University (BUAA) and my PhD from School of Computing Department, National University of Singapore. I am generally interested in Bayesian machine learning, Gaussian process, Bayesian optimization, meta learning and recommendation systems.
