Bio

Hello, my name is Haibin YU, a 5-th year PhD student at the School of Computing Department , National University of Singapore . It is my great honor to be co-supervised by Prof. Kian Hsiang Low and Prof. Patrick Jaillet . I obtained my bachelor degree in Mechanical Engineering and Automation Department, Beihang University (BUAA). My research interests include Bayesian non-parametric methods: Gaussian Process, Bayesian Optimization, Generative Models and Bayesian Deep Learning. My CV can be found [here]
